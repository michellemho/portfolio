About

Michelle has been a Data Scientist on the Research Team of CARTO, a location intelligence and mapping platform, and a Technology Analyst for the Manhattan District Attorney in the Trial Division. She is a recent graduate of NYU's Center for Urban Science + Progress (CUSP) program and has an undergraduate degree in economics from Washington University in St. Louis. With data and maps, she explores how to make cities better places to live. When not wrangling data, she is most likely to be found rock climbing or running.
